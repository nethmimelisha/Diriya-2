About

Welcome to Southern Business Cooperative Bank

Southern Business Cooperative Bank

At Southern Business Cooperative Bank, our unwavering dedication is to collaborate with the economically disadvantaged communities residing in the Southern Province of Sri Lanka. We take pride in being a distinctive financial institution with a singular focus: to enhance the quality of life for low-income households across the Southern Province of Sri Lanka.

Our Vision

To envision a brighter future for our members, customers, dedicated employees, and the broader public, we strive to cultivate impactful partnerships that not only fuel the growth of our bank but also enhance the overall quality of life within our community

Our Mission

Our mission is to be a beacon of integrity within the cooperative finance sector, unwaveringly dedicated to delivering superior value to our members and catalyzing economic development across the island. We commit to seizing opportunities and pioneering innovative microfinance solutions while continuously refining and optimizing banking procedures to set new industry standards.
